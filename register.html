Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about registration and the CGM-EHR integration

What information do I need to register?

Healthcare providers will need their professional credentials, current EHR system information, and details about their practice. Patients will need basic personal information, diabetes type, and current CGM device details. Organizations will need facility information and EHR system details.

Is there a cost to register?

Registration is free for patients. Healthcare providers and organizations may have subscription fees based on the integration features and number of patients. Detailed pricing information will be provided during the registration process.

Which CGM devices are compatible with your system?

Our system currently integrates with Dexcom G6/G7, FreeStyle Libre/Libre 2, and Medtronic Guardian devices. We are continuously expanding our compatibility with additional devices.

How is my health data protected?

All health data is encrypted and securely stored in compliance with HIPAA regulations. We implement robust security measures including multi-factor authentication, secure data transmission, and comprehensive data backup protocols.

What EHR systems are compatible with your integration?

Our system integrates with major EHR platforms including Epic, Cerner, Allscripts, MEDITECH, NextGen, and athenahealth. If you use a different EHR system, please contact us to discuss integration options.
