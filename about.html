About Our Project

Learn about our mission to transform diabetic care through technology integration

Our Mission

The CGM-EHR Integration project aims to revolutionize diabetic care management by connecting continuous glucose monitoring data with electronic health records in real-time. Our mission is to improve patient outcomes and streamline healthcare provider workflows through innovative technology integration.

Project Overview

Our solution focuses on integrating continuous glucose monitoring (CGM) data from devices like Dexcom G6/G7 and FreeStyle Libre with electronic health records (EHR) systems, enabling healthcare providers to monitor diabetic patients in real-time and respond quickly to critical changes in glucose levels.

Key Objectives

  • Improve Chronic Disease Management

    Enable healthcare providers to accurately predict potential complications based on real-time and historical glucose data.

  • Enhance Patient Care

    Develop alert systems to optimize insulin injections for individual patients through smart glucose monitoring.

  • Streamline Provider Workflows

    Reduce time spent reviewing patient data and increase clinical capacity through efficient data integration.
