Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about contacting our team

How quickly can I expect a response to my inquiry?

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 1-2 business days. Technical support requests from registered users receive priority response times, usually within 24 hours.

How can I schedule a demo of the CGM-EHR integration?

You can request a demo by selecting "Request a Demo" in the inquiry type field of our contact form. Alternatively, you can email demo@cgm-ehr.com with your preferred date and time, and our team will coordinate with you.

Where can I get support for technical issues?

Registered users can access our dedicated technical support portal at support.cgm-ehr.com. For urgent assistance, please call our technical support hotline at (555) 234-5678, which is available 24/7.

Can I visit your office to discuss implementation in person?

Yes, in-person consultations are available by appointment. Please contact us at least one week in advance to schedule a meeting with our implementation specialists at our headquarters.
