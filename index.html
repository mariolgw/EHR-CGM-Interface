Diabetic Management Through CGM-EHR Integration

Real-time glucose monitoring integrated with electronic health records for improved chronic disease management and better patient outcomes.

For Healthcare Providers For Patients

Key Features

Our solution bridges the gap between continuous glucose monitoring and electronic health records

Real-Time Monitoring

Access glucose readings from CGM devices instantly within EHR systems. Healthcare providers can monitor diabetic patients in a real-world and real-time manner.

Automated Alerts

Receive notifications when readings fall outside established thresholds. Enable timely interventions for dangerous glucose excursions without delay.

Trend Analysis

Visualize glucose patterns over time to inform treatment decisions. Identify patterns that might be missed with traditional periodic monitoring.

Benefits

Better outcomes for patients and improved workflows for providers
